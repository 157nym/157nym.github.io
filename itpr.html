I.P.T.R

Ønym
5 May 2021
Updated on 18 Jun 2025

I.P.T.R is a solo game I made in just 3 days during my first year, for a Game Jam hosted by ICAN.

The theme was Space Opera, and I handled everything — from game design and visuals to programming.

Since it was my very first jam ever, and as a first year just starting out, things went south pretty quickly. It was a fun learning experience, but the game definitely didn’t get close to being playable.

I was still getting familiar with Unity, so this project helped me understand the basics of the engine and start working with C#.
