Kaiser is a bullet hell game made in just 4 days for the Bullet Hell Game Jam.

I made the sound design and music for this project. Four days was a real challenge — I’d never worked with such a tight deadline before, so it was an intense but valuable experience. I also pitched in a bit on the programming side to help get things running smoothly.
